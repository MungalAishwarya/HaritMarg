Welcome to HaritMarg

HaritMarg Farmer Producer Company Ltd. is a farmer-owned organization based in Nanded district, Maharashtra. We work directly with farmers to aggregate quality agricultural produce, ensure fair pricing, and create reliable market linkages.

Scientifically cultivated seedlings and saplings for a healthy and bountiful fresh produce harvest.

We at Om Gayatri nursery, grow the seedlings in a scientifically controlled environment keeping in mind what is right for the crop.

About us
Our Expertise

We specialise in scientifically cultivated seedlings offering high quality produce in relatively short time. Cultivated and grown using chemical-free farming techniques, our seedlings are fortified with high level of nutrients.

EXCEPTIONAL RESEARCH

Our skilled scientific personnel work at the cutting edge of research and bring a lot of research-based thinking to horticulture, besides tonnes of passion, patience and commitment.

SCIENTIFICALLY GROWN

Our seedlings are developed in a scientifically-controlled microclimatic atmosphere for better results. These seedlings offer a high yield and many productivity gains.

GUIDING FARMERS

The sale of seedlings is the beginning of a rewarding relationship with farmers. Thousands of farmers vouch for our timely and correct in-season agronomy advisory throughout the crop lifecycle.
